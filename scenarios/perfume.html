    Your launch checklist

    Step-by-step, in plain English.

    • 📦
      Source ingredients · Brambleberry, BulkApothecary, or Alibaba for fragrance oils & bottles.
    • 🏷️
      Design a label · Free with Canva. Required by law: ingredients list + your contact info.
    • ⚖️
      Check the rules · Cosmetic safety + labeling. (FDA in the US, REACH in the EU.) Get a parent to read with you.
    • 🛒
      Open a store · Etsy, Shopify, or Instagram. Etsy is the easiest if you're under 18 (parent account).
    • 📷
      Shoot 3 photos · Phone camera + natural light. People buy with their eyes.
    • 💰
      Price it right · Cost × 3 minimum. We exported a starter cost sheet for you below.
